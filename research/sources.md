# Sources — LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

## Topic
Research on LinkedIn organic content strategies for B2B SaaS companies,
applied to the context of 100Hires — a recruitment automation platform
targeting SMBs.

## Why this topic
LinkedIn is the primary channel where HR managers and business owners
(100Hires' ideal customers) spend their professional time. Understanding
how top practitioners use organic LinkedIn content to generate pipeline
is directly actionable for this role.

## Selection Criteria
These experts were chosen based on three filters:
1. **They practice what they teach** — all have built real audiences
   or revenue through LinkedIn organic content, not just written about it
2. **They publish recent, original content** — not recyclers of generic advice
3. **They are relevant to B2B SaaS specifically** — not just general
   social media or personal branding coaches
