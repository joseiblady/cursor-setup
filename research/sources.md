# LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS
## Research Sources & Expert Selection

---

## Research Objective

This research project maps the current landscape of LinkedIn organic 
content strategy as practiced by B2B SaaS companies. The goal is to 
identify repeatable frameworks, content formats, and distribution 
tactics that consistently generate pipeline — not just impressions.

The findings will be directly applicable to companies like 100Hires, 
a recruitment automation SaaS whose ideal customers (HR managers, 
founders, and operations leads at SMBs) are active LinkedIn users 
making high-consideration purchasing decisions.

The central question driving this research:
> What does it actually take to build an organic LinkedIn presence 
> that moves B2B buyers from awareness to conversation — without 
> relying on paid distribution?

---

## Methodology

Experts were identified through a multi-pass selection process:

- **Primary filter:** Does this person generate real business outcomes 
  (pipeline, revenue, inbound leads) through their own LinkedIn presence? 
  Commentators and aggregators were excluded.
- **Secondary filter:** Is their content specific to B2B SaaS contexts, 
  or does it translate directly to them? Generic social media advice 
  was excluded.
- **Tertiary filter:** Are they publishing original thinking based on 
  lived experience, or recycling consensus? Only practitioners with 
  a distinct point of view were included.

The result is a deliberately non-obvious list. Household names in 
marketing were only included when their frameworks are genuinely 
foundational — not for name recognition.

**Why he's on this list:**

rewards before studying how practitioners execute on top of it.
